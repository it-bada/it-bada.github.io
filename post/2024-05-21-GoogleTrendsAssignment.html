When we analyze the Google Trends data for TikTok and Instagram, we can track the growth of their popularity over time. Both platforms experienced significant spikes in interest upon their creation, with TikTok initially having a period of dormancy in terms of Google searches. Instagram followed a similar pattern, but as it was established much earlier, we observe its steady rise in popularity over time.
